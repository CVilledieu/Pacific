## Possible game outlines

### New Gambler to town

You just got ran out of the previous town you lived in and have to start over again. You head back to your home town where your parents set you up with a run down house. It's time to gamble your way to a better life. 

- Meet the towns people gamble with them to run them out of town.
- Head to the town center where you can gamble people out of town to earn money for you and the town

Watch as your town grows
